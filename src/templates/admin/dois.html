How DOI Discovery works

  1. Fetch — Click "Fetch next 20" to query Crossref for papers that don't yet have a DOI. Starts from the most recent papers up to end of 2023 and works backwards (older papers are more likely to have been published). Use "Date range" for a custom window.
  2. Auto-approve — Matches with confidence ≥ 85% are automatically approved. Lower-confidence matches appear in the Pending tab for manual review.
  3. Review — Compare the arXiv paper title/authors against the Crossref match. Approve correct matches to assign the DOI; reject incorrect ones.
  4. Provenance — DOIs are tracked as arxiv (from arXiv metadata), auto (Crossref auto-approved), or verified (admin-approved).
