Score

Relevance weight from 1 to 10. A paper's relevance score sums the scores of all its matched keywords. Higher score = this keyword contributes more to ranking.

Phrase

The canonical display name for this keyword — shown on paper pages and used for filtering. Both the phrase and all its aliases are searched in title and abstract during tagging; all matches are stored under this phrase.

Aliases

Alternate strings that also trigger this keyword during tagging — e.g. plural forms, abbreviations, or spelling variants (schur functions, schur fn). All aliases map to the same canonical phrase. Add new aliases via the Candidates page or the + alias button.

URL

Optional link to a reference page for this keyword (e.g. a page on symmetricfunctions.com). When set, the keyword tag on paper pages becomes a clickable link.
